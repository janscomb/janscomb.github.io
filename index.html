A Quick History

I began my career in Minneapolis in 1996 with Internet Five, helping create one of the first CMS platforms before moving through a series of agency evolutions focused on new media and design. After co-founding a non-profit-focused design firm and launching my own LLC, I expanded my skills into marketing, branding, and print media during the early 2000s. My return to digital work with Implex.net introduced me to Human-Centered Design through interactive and Flash-based experiences, fueling a passion for creative problem-solving. Later, I transitioned into the healthcare tech space with PinpointCare, leading patient experience innovations during the ACA transformation and contributing to chronic disease management platforms. From there, I drove patient engagement initiatives at Accretive Health and worked on benefits administration solutions at bswift under Aetna, focusing on user-centered journeys for a diverse population.
